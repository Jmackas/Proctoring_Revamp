Please do not save anything personal or sensitive when accessing the remote laboratories. The contents of the laboratory are wiped at frequent intervals.

Data exported from the remote laboratories runs the potential of experiencing infection via malicious third party entities. We cannot guarantee the integrity of any data that originates/passes through the laboratories.

Do not install any third party software on a laboratory computer without the permission of an EIT staff member.

Do not copy and potential malicious files or software onto the remote labs. Any attempt to do so could result in punitive measures.

If you notice anything unusual with the laboratory, please contact an EIT staff member immediately.
